🚌 Public transport, reinvented

Move smarter

MOBi is an upcoming mobility companion for public transport in Greece — real-time routes, live arrivals, and seamless trip planning. The app is not available yet.

🗺️

Live Maps

Track buses, metro lines, and trams on a clean interactive map that refreshes every few seconds. See exactly where your vehicle is — no more wondering if it’s around the corner.

⏱️

Exact Arrivals

Get precise, live arrival times for every stop on every line in your city. Say goodbye to guessing and plan your walk to the stop with confidence.

🔔

Smart Alerts

Receive timely notifications for service delays, line disruptions, and when your bus is approaching. MOBi pings you so you don’t have to keep checking.

🌙

Dark Mode

MOBi is designed for day and night. A built-in dark theme reduces eye strain during late commutes and looks great on OLED displays.

📴

Offline Access

Download your city’s routes and timetables locally. Navigate the network even when you have no signal underground or in a dead zone.

♿

Accessibility

Full screen-reader support, high-contrast mode, and large-text options ensure every commuter — regardless of ability — can travel with confidence.
